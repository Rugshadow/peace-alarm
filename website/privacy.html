  • We collect personal information you provide (email, username, password) and information automatically collected (device data, usage data).

  • We do not collect information from third parties.
  • We process your information to provide and improve our services, communicate with you, and comply with law.
  • We do not sell or share your personal information.

Personal information you provide:

We do not process sensitive information.

Application data we may collect:

Information automatically collected:

  • Log and usage data (IP address, device info, browser type, pages viewed, date/time stamps)

We process your information to:
