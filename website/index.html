Wake up better.

Pick from music, news, comedy, ambient, motivational, religious, educational, storytelling, health, or alternative alarms.

Rotating Daily

One channel could have hundreds or even thousands of alarms, so you never have to wake up to the same sound twice.

Record Your Own

Turn on creator mode and create alarms to share with others, like a mini morning podcast or tune!

🔒

Reliable

Alarms play in silent mode just like your phone's default alarm app. Favorite channels can be saved offline, and if the audio can't be reached, there is a selection of fallback alarm sounds to choose from.

🌐

Available in 15 Languages

Alarms that feature the spoken word are available in English, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Russian, Arabic, Hindi, Bengali, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, Vietnamese, Indonesian, and Filipino.

💸

Completely Free

How It Works

Three steps to
better mornings

1

Browse Channels

Explore your favorite channels and listen all you like. Save your favorites to be used as alarms.

2

Set Your Alarms

Choose as many alarms as you'd like, then set the time, day, recursion, and of course the alarm channel you want.

3

Wake Up Better

When your alarm fires, it will play audio from your selected channel. Choose in settings if you like it played in order, newest always, or shuffled.
